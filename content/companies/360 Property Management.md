# What's Good, Great, Bad, and Ugly About 360 Property Management

## What's Good About the Property Management Company

360 Property Management, based on a series of reviews, exhibits several positive aspects:

1. **Responsive Maintenance**: Many reviewers have highlighted the company's prompt response to repair requests. This demonstrates their commitment to ensuring the properties they manage are well-maintained and comfortable for tenants.

2. **Effective Communication**: Some reviewers have commended 360 Property Management for their communication skills. They have noted that the company is efficient at keeping tenants informed and addressing their concerns.

3. **Friendly and Helpful Staff**: Multiple reviews mention the friendly and helpful nature of the staff. A special shout-out was given to one staff member, Bronwyn, who provided impeccable customer service. Such positive interactions can make a significant difference in the rental experience.

4. **Assistance During Difficult Times**: One reviewer shared how 360 Property Management went the extra mile to help them during a challenging period, preventing them from becoming homeless. This level of assistance and understanding reflects a compassionate approach to property management.

## What's Great About the Property Management Company

Apart from the good aspects, some reviewers had even more positive experiences with 360 Property Management:

1. **Professionalism**: Reviewers describe the company as professional and polite, making the entire move-in process a smooth and positive experience.

2. **Customer Advocacy**: In several reviews, it is mentioned that the company advocates for tenants when it comes to repairs and maintenance. This level of support can greatly benefit renters and enhance their overall living conditions.

3. **Responsive to Vendor Needs**: A vendor who worked with 360 Property Management praised the team for their hard work, not only for tenants but also for property owners and vendors. This highlights the company's commitment to all aspects of property management.

4. **Convenient Payment System**: The availability of an app for rent payment is acknowledged as a convenient feature, making it easier for tenants to manage their financial commitments.

5. **Improvement Over Time**: Some reviewers note that the company has improved over time, suggesting a commitment to enhancing their services and meeting tenant needs.

## What's Bad About the Property Management Company

Despite the positive reviews, there are some areas where 360 Property Management falls short:

1. **Issues with Property Condition**: Multiple reviews mentioned problems with property conditions, including outdated apartments, maintenance issues, and fixtures in disrepair. This indicates room for improvement in maintaining and updating their properties.
