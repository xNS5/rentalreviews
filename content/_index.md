Finding a rental property can be a challenge -- especially for those who plan on living on their own for the first time. While this is a big step forward for many, companies can and do take advantage of that for their own financial benefit. Luckily, there are platforms out there which allow users to submit reviews of both property management companies and properties themselves. While the existence of these platforms is great, it's often challenging to get a well-rounded picture based on those reviews. 

This project seeks to summarize reviews gathered from Google, Yelp, and Reddit to provide an overall summary of property management companies and apartment complexes by listing out the good, great, bad, and ugly aspects of renting from specific companies.
